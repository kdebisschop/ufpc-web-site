Book Club

Our book club has a fun 2016- 2017 book club season planned.  Please find our updated schedule below!

Anybody is welcome to join us, and we make it our goal to read as wide a variety of books as we can. So if you enjoy reading, and would like to share that pleasure with some like-minded friends, come join us for discussion and snacks Tuesday night from 7:00 to 8:30. All are advised to bring their own beverages.

2017 Book Group Schedule

September 5 - The Secret Life of Lobsters: How Fishermen and Scientists Are Unraveling the Mysteries of Our Favorite Crustacean by Trevor Corson

October 3 - The Elegance of the Hedgehog by Muriel Barbery

November 7 - The American Spirit: Who We Are and What We Stand For by David McCullough

January 2 - Free Women, Free Men: Sex, Gender, Feminism by Camille Paglia

February 6 - The Rosie Project: A Novel by Graeme Simsion

March 6 - A Man Called Ove: A Novel by Fredrik Backman

April 3 - One of Ours (free version) by Willa Cather

May 1 - The Handmaid's Tale by Margaret Atwood

June 5 - The Reason I Jump: The Inner Voice of a Thirteen-Year-Old Boy with Autism by Naoki Higashida
